Crispy Chicken Tenders

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenders
  • Panko
  • Chili Powder
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Egg Whites
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Clean Chicken Tenders with Vinegar and Water
  2. Mix Flour with Cornstarch
  3. Dip Chicken Tenders in dry mixture, egg whites, and then panko mixed with all your seasoning.
  4. Place in Air Fryer and spray with cooking spray on each side.
  5. Air Fry for 9-10 minutes on 180C until golden brown.
