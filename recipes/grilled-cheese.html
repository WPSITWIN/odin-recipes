Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Low Carb White Bread
  • Reduced Fat Sliced Cheese
  • Low Fat Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat your pan to medium heat
  2. Get two slices of bread and even spread butter on both sides of each slice.
  3. Place one slice of cheese between the butter bread.
  4. Place onto the pan.
  5. Flip after 2-3 mins.
  6. Grilled until golden brown on both sides.
